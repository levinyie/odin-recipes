Taro Milk Tea

Description

Milk Tea infused with Taro, creating a slightly creamy texture with an aromatic sweetness.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Taro Powder
  • Sugar/Syrup
  • Ice

Steps

  1. First heat up the milk to just below boiling. You do not want to burn the milk
  2. Then add 2 tablespoons of taro powder into the milk and stir
  3. Once mixed, add sugar/syrup based on preference.
  4. Once everything is mixed, add the ice and stir until the drink has cooled.
