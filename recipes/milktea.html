Milk Tea

Description

Black tea served with milk and sugar, creating a classic beverage commonly consumed around the world.

Ingredients

  • Black Tea
  • Milk
  • Brown Sugar
  • Ice (Optional)

Steps

  1. First boil a cup of black tea
  2. When the black tea is boiled, add 2 teaspoons of brown sugar (Sweetness based on preference)
  3. Stir until brown sugar is fully disolved
  4. Add milk and stir
  5. Ready to drink hot, if you prefer it iced, add ice afterwards and stir until the drink has cooled
